About PLM

PLM, established in 1965 by Republic Act No. 4196, is the first chartered and autonomous university funded by a city government. Opening its doors on July 17, 1967, PLM welcomed 556 students to its campus in Manila's historic Intramuros district, known for its Spanish colonial heritage. Today, over 10,000 students benefit from PLM's tuition-free education. PLM holds historical significance, built on the former sites of prestigious educational institutions like Universidad de San Ignacio and Ateneo de Municipal de Manila. It's recognized for its 100% licensure exam pass rate and commitment to producing socially responsible graduates since its inception in 1967
