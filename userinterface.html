About PLM

The Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM) will start accepting online applications for freshmen students (undergraduate programs) for Academic Year (AY) 2024-2025 beginning on December 1, 2023.

For AY 2024-2025, there will be an on-site PLM Admission Test (PLMAT). Minimum health protocols will be observed to ensure the safety of student-applicants. The PLMAT is a 4-hour examination consisting of sub-tests in English, Science, Mathematics, Filipino, and Abstract Reasoning. Passing the PLMAT is one of the requirements for admission to PLM.

Applicants are encouraged to prepare for the PLMAT by reviewing relevant subjects and familiarizing themselves with the exam format. Additionally, detailed guidelines and application procedures will be available on the official PLM website.

The application period provides a crucial opportunity for aspiring students to join one of the country's premier institutions, offering a range of undergraduate programs designed to foster academic excellence and professional development. For further inquiries, applicants may contact the PLM admissions office through their official channels.

Who may Apply

Student-applicants may apply provided that they comply with any of the following basic qualifications:

1. Senior High School (SHS) student who is currently enrolled in Grade 12 at a Department of Education (DepEd) accredited senior high school with a Grade 11 general weighted average (GWA) of 80 or above.

2. Senior High School graduate from a DepEd accredited senior high school with a Grade 11 general weighted average (GWA) of 80 or above and has not taken any college or university units or programs during the application period.

3. Alternative Learning System (ALS) completer who has passed the Accreditation & Equivalency (A&E) as recommended for tertiary education and has not taken any college or university units/programs during the application period.

Applicants who meet these qualifications are invited to take the first step towards becoming part of the PLM community, known for its commitment to providing quality education and producing graduates who excel in their chosen fields.
